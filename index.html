Holiday Program
Holiday Programs: Academic Catch up / Fun Club (Subject to availability)

The aim of the holiday program includes providing constructive activities for children in both academia- catch up programs as well as social, skill acquisition and leisure programs.

School holiday programs have Incredible Benefits and are great for children to develop leadership, social and physical skills. They help children learn about team building, working in groups and interacting with others in a different environment. It provides children with safe places to play, gives them opportunities to learn new skills, unwind from the daily stresses, review concepts and engage in a range of new experiences.
